# üö® WARNING SIGNS & INTERVENTION PLAN

## Red Flags to Watch For:

### Physical/Health Concerns:
- Frequent complaints of feeling sick before activities
- Changes in sleep patterns or appetite
- Increased injuries or physical complaints
- Regression in toilet training or other skills

**Action:** Reduce activity load immediately; consult pediatrician if persists.

---

### Emotional/Behavioral Concerns:
- Crying or tantrums about attending activities
- Withdrawal or sadness
- Increased aggression or behavioral issues at home/school
- Nightmares or anxiety

**Action:** Pause one or more activities; consider professional support.

---

### Schedule/Family Stress:
- Family missing meals together regularly
- Parents feeling overwhelmed or resentful
- Siblings negatively impacted
- No downtime or free play for Reyansh

**Action:** Immediately scale back to 1-2 activities; reassess priorities.

---

### Financial Strain:
- Difficulty paying for activities
- Cutting into emergency savings
- Family conflict about costs
- Stress about upcoming payments

**Action:** Drop to free/low-cost alternatives; reassess budget.

---

## Intervention Protocol:

**Step 1: Identify the Issue**
- What specifically is concerning?
- When did it start?
- Is it getting better or worse?

**Step 2: Immediate Action**
- Take 1-week break from activities if severe
- Reduce to bare minimum if moderate
- Consult professionals if health/safety concern

**Step 3: Root Cause Analysis**
- Is it the activity itself?
- Is it the instructor/environment?
- Is it the schedule/timing?
- Is it overall overwhelm?

**Step 4: Adjust or Pivot**
- Drop specific problematic activity, OR
- Reduce frequency across the board, OR
- Switch to different activity type, OR
- Take extended break (1-2 months)

**Step 5: Rebuild Gradually**
- Start with one favorite activity only
- Add back slowly (one per month)
- Monitor continuously
- Prioritize Reyansh's wellbeing over goals
